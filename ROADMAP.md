# Yesat NGO Website - Development Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the strategic development of the **Yesat** youth-led NGO website. Our goal is to create a professional, immersive, and high-performance digital presence that resonates with funders and community stakeholders.

## Technical Foundation
- **Design Palette**: Elegant Cream (#FFFDD0 / #F5F5DC contrast)
- **State Management**: [Riverpod 3](https://riverpod.dev) (Standard Providers, No Generators)
- **Routing**: [ZenRouter](https://pub.dev/packages/zenrouter) (Manual Config, No Generators)
- **Animations**: Custom Scroll Transitions & Slide Effects
